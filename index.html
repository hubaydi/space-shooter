Space Shooter — Free Online Arcade Shooter Game

Space Shooter is a free browser-based arcade shooter inspired by the classic space-invaders genre. Pilot a neon starship through an endless onslaught of alien invaders, dodge incoming fire, and blast your way through waves of escalating difficulty. There's nothing to download and no account to create — just load the page and start playing in seconds on any desktop or mobile device.

Every wave brings faster, tougher enemies and tighter spawn rates, so only sharp reflexes and smart use of power-ups will keep you alive. Compete for your personal best — your high score is saved automatically in your browser — and push to beat it run after run.

How to Play Space Shooter

The controls are simple and work on both keyboard and touch screens:

  • Move: Arrow keys or WASD on desktop; on-screen D-pad on mobile.
  • Shoot: Press Space on desktop; tap the FIRE button on mobile.
  • Pause: Press P on desktop or tap the pause button (II).
  • Mute: Press M to toggle sound effects on or off.

You start each run with 3 lives and a brief moment of invincibility after taking a hit. Collect floating power-ups dropped by destroyed enemies to gain an edge, and survive as long as you can to climb the wave counter and rack up your score.

Power-Ups Explained

Destroyed enemies occasionally drop one of three temporary power-ups:

  • Rapid — Greatly increases your fire rate so you can lay down a wall of bullets.
  • Spread — Fires multiple bullets in a spread pattern to cover a wider area.
  • Shield — Grants temporary invincibility, letting you plow through enemies danger-free.

Power-ups last for a limited time, so time your grabs to survive the most dangerous moments of a wave. Mix and match: a Spread + Rapid combo is devastating against tanky groups.

Tips for a Higher Score

  • Keep moving — staying in one place makes you an easy target for wandering invaders.
  • Prioritise Fast enemies; they reach you quickly and slip through gaps.
  • Use the Shield power-up to dive into a cluster and clear a crowded screen.
  • Don't waste bullets on a lone enemy when a Spread pickup can double your coverage.
  • Learn the sine-wave drift of enemies so you can anticipate where they'll be.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Space Shooter free to play?

Yes. Space Shooter is 100% free to play directly in your web browser. There is no download, no installation, and no sign-up required.

How do I play Space Shooter?

On desktop, move with the Arrow keys or WASD and shoot with the Spacebar. Press P to pause and M to mute. On touch devices, use the on-screen D-pad and FIRE button. Collect power-ups to boost your firepower.

Does Space Shooter work on mobile phones?

Yes. The game is fully responsive and automatically shows on-screen touch controls — a D-pad, FIRE button, and pause button — on phones and tablets.

Is my high score saved?

Yes. Your best score is stored locally in your browser, so it persists between sessions on the same device and browser.

What power-ups are in the game?

Three power-ups: Rapid Fire for faster shooting, Spread Shot for multi-directional bullets, and Shield for temporary invincibility.

What types of enemies are there?

Enemies come in three types: Normal invaders, Fast invaders that are quick but fragile, and Tank invaders that are slow but take more hits. Tanks start appearing from wave 2 and become more common as the waves rise.
